19L Ads

If you would like to put your ad on our site, send an email to ads@19lgames.tk

Example Ad

The email must contain:

  • A title for your ad
  • A link for your ad to go to
  • An 300x300px image for your ad

We will review your ad

If it passes the review, we will put it on some of the webpages for 10 days.

After the 10 days, you can submit a different or the same ad again

You may only have 1 ad at a time

These ads will be free
